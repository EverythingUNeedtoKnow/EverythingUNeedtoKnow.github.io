TOWNSHIP

1.  Planting/Harvesting Wheat is the Cheapest Way to Earn XP; Be sure to Spread out your Farming Goods Across the Fields

>  You can easily rack up a ton of coins just by planting/harvesting a bunch of wheat, the proceeding to sell it out of your barn. It's also easy to farm XP points by planting /harvesting a ton of wheat, because it for free.

2. Make sure You have Enough coins and Citizens Before You build something New

>  You'll always need coins in order to build something new, so make sure you have enough in the bank.

>  A good strategy to adopt is building a bunch of house so you can quickly gather more citizens and build up a few of those super beneficial Special buildings.

3.  Set up Activites with long Completion Times before you Log Off

>  Whenever you feel like you're done for the day, set up a few buildings with long completion times tied to them before you go. That way, you'll return to a town full of completed buildings.

4. Befriend a Ton of Players So you can Send/Receive Gifts and Acquire Help at the Train Station

>  It's worth befreinding a bunch of Township players, especially the ones you notice are most active.

5. Always Make Sure you Login for Five Consecutive Days to Claim Your Daily Bonus Rewards

>  Township's "Daily Bonus" comes your way simply by playing it five days ina row. The first four days fill up your coins purse, then the fifth and the final day throws an awesome gift your way.
