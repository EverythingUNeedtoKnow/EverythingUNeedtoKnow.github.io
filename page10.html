  1. Pay attention to the Team Setup Recommendations for each Mission - Try to add someone who can utilize healing abilities. That way, you can back up your two recommended Heroes with a character who can keep them alive as they take on the bulk of the current mission's tasks. Always rely on your strongest and rarest Heroes from each role when you're constructing your team. 
  2. Be mindful of your Heroes' Skills before you take them on a Mission - As you're selecting a Hero who's role syncs up with the Team Recommendations, read up on their stats and skills. Specialists are especially useful to your team - they tend to stick to one or two of your Heroes who have Stat boosting abilities.
  3. Craft only one type of a piece of armor or weapon - That is because, instead of wasting your time and money on new gear for every Hero, equip them before going on a mission and then after completing the mission, quickly remove everything; so you can equip the gear to another Hero for another mission.
  4. Assign the right Heroes to Specific rooms to boost their Productivity and Put your HQ to work while You work
  5. Make it a habit of completing your Daily Missions and Participating in Events
  6. And, Stay on Top of your Achievements!
